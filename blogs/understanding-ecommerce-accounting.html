Understanding eCommerce Accounting: A Complete Guide for Online Sellers

By Team Ledgerly • 15 Oct 2025

Accounting for eCommerce sellers goes beyond basic bookkeeping. With multiple marketplaces, payment gateways, and returns, maintaining accurate books requires a structured approach. Start by automating sales data imports, categorizing marketplace fees, and regularly reconciling bank statements. Cloud-based accounting tools integrated with Amazon, Flipkart, or Shopify can help you minimize manual effort while ensuring compliance.
