Apparel Seller – Proactive Trademark Protection

Strategic trademark filing enabled Amazon Brand Registry and 36x ROI

Trademark Registration + Amazon Brand Registry Enablement

Client Profile

  • Business: Ethnic women's wear - Brand Name: EthnicElegance - Stage: Planning Amazon launch - Previous Experience: ₹15L via Instagram & WhatsApp - Objective: Protected Amazon launch on Amazon

The Challenge

Sanya had been successfully selling her ethnic wear designs through Instagram and WhatsApp for 18 months, generating ₹15 lakhs in revenue. Now she wanted to scale by launching on Amazon, but faced critical requirements:

Amazon Brand Registry requires a registered trademark, but trademark registration in India takes 18–24 months — creating a growth vs protection dilemma.

Amazon Brand Registry Requirements:

  • Amazon requires trademark registration (not just application) for Brand Registry
  • Brand Registry essential for:
    • ASIN ownership and control
    • A+ Content (enhanced product pages)
    • Sponsored Brands advertising
    • Brand Analytics access
    • Protection against counterfeit sellers
    • Premium brand positioning

The Dilemma:

  • Trademark registration takes 18-24 months in India
  • Can't access Brand Registry benefits without registered trademark
  • Risk of starting without protection:
    • Other sellers could list under her brand name
    • No control over ASIN listings
    • Limited advertising options
    • Vulnerable to copycats once brand gains traction
  • But waiting 2 years means losing market opportunity

Competitive Pressure:

  • Similar brands already established on Amazon
  • Market getting crowded in ethnic wear category
  • Needed to launch soon to capture market share
  • But wanted to do it RIGHT with full protection

"I knew I had to get on Amazon to scale, but I also heard horror stories of brands getting hijacked by copycats. Friends told me to just start selling and worry about trademark later, but I wanted to build a real, protected brand from day one."

Our Solution - Strategic Phased Approach**

Phase 1: Comprehensive Trademark Filing (Month 1)

Trademark Search & Strategy:

  • Conducted thorough trademark database search
  • Confirmed "EthnicElegance" available in relevant classes
  • Recommended filing in 3 classes:
    • Class 25: Clothing, footwear, headgear
    • Class 35: Retail services, online retail of clothing
    • Class 24: Textiles, fabric (future expansion consideration)

Application Filing:

  • Prepared comprehensive trademark applications:
    • Word mark: "EthnicElegance"
    • Logo/device mark: Brand name + distinctive design element
  • Filed with Indian Trade Marks Registry
  • Obtained application numbers within 1 week

Documentation Support:

  • Prepared proper specimen and representations
  • Documented brand usage evidence (Instagram, packaging, invoices)
  • Set up proper record-keeping for future renewals

Phase 2: Initial Amazon Launch (Month 2-6)

Started with Basic Seller Account:

  • Created Amazon Seller Central account
  • Listed products without Brand Registry (initial phase)
  • Used ™ symbol (application pending)
  • Built initial sales traction and reviews

Built Foundation While Waiting:

  • Professional product photography
  • Optimized product descriptions
  • Gathered customer reviews and testimonials
  • Built email list and social media following
  • Documented all brand-building activities

Monitored Trademark Progress:

  • Regular follow-ups with Trade Marks Registry
  • Responded promptly to any examination reports
  • Maintained organized documentation

Phase 3: Trademark Prosecution (Month 7-18)

Navigated Registration Process:

Month 7: Trademark Examination

  • Received examination report (minor objections)
  • Prepared detailed reply addressing concerns
  • Submitted additional evidence of use

Month 10: Publication in Trade Marks Journal

  • Trademark published for opposition
  • Monitored for any opposition filings
  • No opposition received (4-month window)

Month 15: Registration Processing

  • Application moved to registration stage
  • Final documentation submitted
  • Certificate generation process initiated

Month 18: Trademark Registration Granted

  • Received trademark registration certificate
  • Now authorized to use ® symbol
  • Full legal protection secured

Phase 4: Amazon Brand Registry Activation (Month 19-20)

Immediate Enrollment:

Week 1-2: Brand Registry Application

  • Applied for Amazon Brand Registry
  • Submitted:
    • Trademark registration certificate
    • Brand logo and images
    • Product catalog
    • Website/social media presence proof
  • Verified brand ownership

Week 3: Approval & Enrollment

  • Brand Registry approved
  • Account upgraded with full features
  • Team trained on new tools

Unlocked Premium Features:

Content Enhancement:

  • Created A+ Content for all product listings
    • Enhanced images and infographics
    • Brand story section
    • Comparison charts
    • Professional layout

Advertising Expansion:

  • Launched Sponsored Brands campaigns
    • Custom headline and logo
    • Showcase multiple products
    • Drive to brand store
  • Access to Brand Analytics
  • Video in search ads capability

Brand Protection:

  • Set up automated brand protection
  • Proactive monitoring for trademark violations
  • Report-a-violation tools activated
  • ASIN ownership confirmed

Phase 5: Long-term Brand Building (Ongoing)

Created Brand Store:

  • Custom Amazon storefront
  • Multiple product categories organized
  • Brand story and values communicated
  • Enhanced customer experience

Expanded Product Line:

  • Launched new designs confidently
  • All products protected under trademark
  • Consistent brand identity across range
  • Built product family under umbrella brand
