Food Products Seller - Multi-State GST Setup

GST Registration + FSSAI + Multi-State Compliance

Client Profile

  • Business: Organic snacks and healthy foods
  • Founder: Former corporate professional turned entrepreneur
  • Stage: 6 months old, registered in Karnataka
  • Challenge: Expanding to sell in 5+ states

The Challenge

Rajesh had successfully built a local customer base selling organic snacks through his website and local stores in Bangalore. When he listed on Amazon and Flipkart, orders started coming from all over India. However:

  • Only had GST registration in Karnataka (principal place of business)
  • Tax liability exceeding ₹20 lakhs in Maharashtra, Delhi, Tamil Nadu
  • Required additional GST registrations in these states
  • FSSAI license was state-specific, needed central license
  • Receiving notices for non-registration in multiple states
  • Managing compliance across states was becoming a nightmare

"I got my first GST notice from Maharashtra for ₹3.2 lakhs. I didn't even know I needed to register there separately!"

Our Solution

Immediate Crisis Management (Week 1)

  • Analyzed sales data across all states for past 6 months
  • Identified states where turnover exceeded ₹20 lakhs threshold:
    • Maharashtra: ₹28 lakhs
    • Delhi: ₹24 lakhs
    • Tamil Nadu: ₹22 lakhs
  • Filed for GST registration in all 3 states on urgent basis
  • Prepared detailed reply to Maharashtra GST notice explaining:
    • Genuine oversight (not willful default)
    • Immediate registration action taken
    • Voluntary disclosure of other states

Compliance Restructuring (Week 2-4)

  • Upgraded FSSAI State License to Central License (mandatory for multi-state sellers)
  • Set up proper accounting systems to track state-wise sales
  • Implemented monthly state-wise GST review process
  • Registered for additional states as precautionary measure:
    • Uttar Pradesh (sales growing rapidly)
    • Gujarat (approaching threshold)

Ongoing Compliance Management

  • Monthly GST return filing across 6 states
  • State-wise sales monitoring with threshold alerts
  • E-way bill management for interstate supplies
  • Annual GST reconciliation across all states

The Results

Immediate Outcomes:

✅ All state GST registrations obtained within 30 days
✅ Central FSSAI license obtained
✅ Zero further compliance notices

Business Impact:

  • Confidently expanded to all-India operations
  • Revenue grew from ₹8 lakhs/month to ₹28 lakhs/month
  • Saved estimated ₹8+ lakhs in potential penalties
  • Can now focus on marketing instead of compliance fires

Financial Impact:

  • Before: Facing ₹3.2L demand + potential ₹5L+ in other states
  • After: Settled for ₹45K + fully compliant across India
  • Savings: ₹7.5+ lakhs in penalties avoided
