GST/Indirect Taxes

Comprehensive GST and indirect tax advisory covering compliance, planning, litigation support, and international trade documentation.

Overview

GST and indirect tax management requires specialized expertise to ensure compliance while optimizing tax positions. Our comprehensive GST/Indirect Taxes practice provides end-to-end support across all aspects of goods and services taxation. We offer strategic GST advisory including registration, return filing, reconciliation, input tax credit optimization, and compliance management. Our services extend to handling GST notices, assessments, audits, and appellate proceedings. We provide transaction structuring advice to minimize tax impact while maintaining full compliance with GST laws. For businesses engaged in international trade, we offer customs and duty advisory, import-export documentation, DGFT compliance, and duty drawback claims. Our team stays current with frequent regulatory changes, providing proactive guidance that protects your interests while ensuring seamless operations across multiple states and jurisdictions.
