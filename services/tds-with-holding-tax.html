TDS / With Holding Tax

Comprehensive management and deposit of Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) and related filings.

Overview

This service covers the statutory requirements of TDS/TCS (Tax Collected at Source) compliance, including calculating the correct amount to be withheld, timely deposit of the tax with the government, and filing of quarterly TDS returns (e.g., Form 24Q, 26Q). We manage the issuance of TDS certificates (Form 16/16A) to deductees. Proper management of TDS is crucial for businesses to avoid penalties and interest, ensuring they fulfill their obligations as a tax deductor/collector under the Income Tax Act.
