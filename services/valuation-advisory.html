Valuation Advisory

Professional startup valuation using industry-standard methodologies providing defensible numbers for fundraising, M&A, and compliance.

Overview

Accurate business valuation is critical for fundraising, mergers and acquisitions, ESOP pricing, and compliance requirements. Our Valuation Advisory services provide professionally prepared valuations using recognized methodologies that stand up to investor scrutiny and regulatory requirements. We employ multiple valuation approaches including Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis, comparable company analysis (trading comps), precedent transaction analysis (M&A comps), and venture capital method for early-stage startups. Our valuations consider your business model, growth trajectory, market size, competitive positioning, financial performance, and risk factors. For regulatory purposes, we provide valuations compliant with Companies Act (for ESOPs, buybacks, M&A), Income Tax Act (for capital gains), and FEMA regulations (for foreign investment). Our comprehensive valuation reports include detailed financial modeling, assumption documentation, and sensitivity analysis—providing stakeholders with clear rationale and defensible numbers that support successful transactions.
