Transaction Advisory Service

Comprehensive transaction advisory covering M&A, restructuring, IPOs, and cross-border deals with financial, tax, and regulatory expertise.

Overview

Complex business transactions require integrated advisory covering financial, tax, legal, and regulatory aspects. Our Transaction Advisory Services provide end-to-end support for mergers, acquisitions, restructuring, IPOs, and international transactions—ensuring successful execution while protecting client interests. We provide comprehensive transaction support including M&A advisory (buy-side and sell-side), business valuation and deal structuring, financial and tax due diligence, transaction structuring for tax efficiency, regulatory compliance and approval management, and post-transaction integration support. For cross-border deals, we handle FEMA compliance, withholding tax, and international structuring. Our multidisciplinary approach combines technical expertise across finance, taxation, and compliance with strong transaction execution skills. We help identify suitable opportunities, conduct thorough due diligence, structure deals optimally, negotiate favorable terms, and ensure smooth closing—maximizing value while managing risks throughout the transaction lifecycle.
